Our Story

As told by Kara

Aaron and I met in the fall of 2013 during my first quarter at Western Washington University. My first quarter of Computer Science was difficult and I needed extra help so Aaron offered to tutor me. We ended up spending most days after school together and we got to know each other really well. After that first fall quarter we made our relationship official.

Boyfriend & Girlfriend

December 8th, 2013

One evening in the spring quarter of 2015 I was sitting in class after a long and tiring day, when Aaron sent a text asking if I wanted to go for a drive. Of course I did, so after my class Aaron picked me up at school and we drove to Deception Pass. As we drove Aaron talked about how it was a special place for him. He pointed out spots as we drove that reminded him of his childhood and, eventually we made it to the beach. We sat down on a piece of driftwood and talked for a little bit until Aaron casually mentioned that my engagement ring had been finished earlier that day. In that instant tears started flowing from my face. Aaron got down on one knee and pulled out the ring. He finally asked if I would marry him and I of course said yes. After I cleaned the tears from my face we took a walk down the beach as the sun set.

Engaged

April 29th, 2015

Now we are planning our wedding and our future together.
