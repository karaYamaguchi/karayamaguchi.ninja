About Me

I am a Web Developer and Designer with experience in several programming languages including, but not limited to, Java, C, Python, and Ruby. I also have experience with HTML, CSS, PHP, and JavaScript.

In addition to programming experience I have graphic design experience using Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop, and Gimp.

Outside of work and school I enjoy spending time with my dog. His name is Sherman and if you're familiar with the Seattle Seahawks you probably know where the inspiration for his name came from. On game days he gets just as excited as we do.

I spend any free time I have either drawing or painting or just using my creativity.

Currently I am the Website Administrator for Western Washington University's Association for Women in Computing.

The AWC website can be found at awc.orgs.cs.wwu.edu.
